My Personal Rig

Mar 7, 2021 • Jonathan Tweedle

In the Previous Post I spoke about the changes to my desk setup to mount my new monitor to help with my posture and ergonomics. That post drew quite some attention to my surprise, with some interest into my personal equipment.

So here is a quick summary of my personal rig. It is a little bit dated, but still gets the job done.

  • Monitor: Samsung G9 Odyssey 49” Dual-QHD 32:9 Ultrawide
  • Graphics Card: EVGA GeForce GTX 1080 SC2 GAMING iCX
  • CPU: Intel Core i7 7700k processor
  • Motherboard: ASUS TUF Z270 Mark 1
  • Memory: Team T-Force DARK 16GB (2 x 8GB) 288-Pin DDR4 SDRAM DDR4 3000
  • Storage: Samsung 970 EVO NVMe M.2 1TB SSD
  • Storage: Samsung 840 EVO SATA III 2.5 inch 250 GB SSD
  • Storage: Seagate Barracuda 1TB 7200 RPM SATA 6gbs
  • PSU: Seasonic FOCUS Plus 550 Platinum SSR-550PX 550W 80+ Platinum ATX12V & EPS12V Full Modular 120mm
  • Case: Huntkey Midi Tower Case
  • Keyboard: Coolermaster Masterkeys Pro S (White) Cherry MX Brown
  • Mouse: Roccat Kone XTD Laser Gaming Mouse
  • Desk Pad: HyperX Fury Gaming Pad and Reflex Lab Silky Smooth 5mm 36 x 12
  • Headset: HyperX Cloud II Gaming Headset

I also bought a simple USB Switch that allows me to switch the input between my Personal Gaming Rig and my work laptop. This really helps save time from constantly having to disconnect and switch cables around.
